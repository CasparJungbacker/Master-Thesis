Cloud Botany sample case

This is run_1 of the Cloud Botany ensemble, center of the parameter hypercube.
for use with DALES to4.4.2_Fredrik or v4.4.1

Settings:
 RRTMG radiation
 bulkmicro microphysics.
 52 and 62 advection schemes


Domain and resolution:
Production runs are done on a grid of 1536x1536 cells horizontally,
with 100m resolution.
